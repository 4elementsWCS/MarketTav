ABOUT US

Opened in the start of 2015, The Market Tavern Bordeaux GastroPub is on the forefront of modern pub food and tasty craft beers on tap. Inspired by the modern gastro-pubs of London, TMT is a small family oriented establishment in the heart of Bordeaux’s chic and historic Chartrons district.

Welcoming you with open arms seven days a week from 11am until 2am, this family owned pub has 40 seats inside and 40 seats on a terrace with table service all day. During lunchtimes you can enjoy the “Formule du Midi” that includes a selection of different starters, main courses and desserts that changes every day using fresh produce.

Send us a message to book a table.
